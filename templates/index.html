Overview

Aeon of the New Republic is a web-based CLI tool that allows users to create a character, much like one would when playing a Role-Playing game, share updates about their achievements for the day and connect with other user groups to form accountability and "mastermind" circles.

A full feature list and documentation can be found here ».

All of our software is free and open-source.

Why use our tool?

Create

Make a profile, join a Republic and develop your creative potential with your peers.

Grow

Work together, share your advancements and level up your creative abilities.

Achieve

Celebrate completion of creative works, prove to yourself that you can do whatever you set your mind to and show others that they can do the same.

Repeat

Why stop there? Use the momentum you already have to start a new project or plan improvements to what you've already created.

Don't forget to take time to reflect first, with some well deserved time outside of your creative venture or a full-blown retrospective write up!

Interested in joining?

Currently, the AotNR community is accessible on an invite-only basis. A unique, limited-time registration URL will be sent to invitees to gather information for a new account on the AotNR platform.

If you know someone who is already part of the AotNR community or would like more information and access, please get in touch via the links at the bottom of the page.

Once the AotNR community is established, registrations will be made open to all - stay tuned!
